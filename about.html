About Us




ShultanGolf is an Indonesian brand for golf apparel established in 2013. Our company has more than 25 years experience in garment manufacturing for both local and international brands. Svingolf manufactures its own golf apparel with the highest quality of fabric and workmanship.

Our commitment is to give our customers with the best quality, most stylish and comfortable golf apparel. Svingolf uses 100% Polyester fabric with quick dry, anti UV, anti microbial and breathable material to ensure that every golfer have “The Perfect Wear For The Perfect Swing”.

Our specialty is to provide our customers with customize design for their golf apparel needs to show the identity of an individual or a company.

As an Indonesian brand, our philosophy is to show Indonesian culture in most of our apparel designs in order to promote Indonesian cultures and products.

Svingolf is a proud sponsor of CIMB Niaga Indonesian Masters the most prestigious golf event in Indonesia for three years in a row.

Vision

Our visions are to be the Indonesian brand for golf apparel and to make our customers proud of Indonesian product.

Mision

Our mission is to work closely with our customers to provide the best solutions for golf apparel with the highest standard.
